What To Do Right Now

1

Change Your UPI PIN Immediately

Open your UPI app (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm, BHIM) → Go to Profile → UPI PIN → Change PIN. Use a unique 6-digit PIN not used elsewhere.

2

Deregister & Re-register Your UPI ID

In your UPI app, navigate to Manage UPI IDs, delete the compromised ID, and create a new one with a different handle.

3

Enable Transaction Limits & Alerts

Set a daily transaction limit in your bank's app and enable SMS/email alerts for every transaction.

4

Check Linked Accounts

Review which bank accounts are linked to this UPI ID. Consider using a dedicated low-balance account for UPI.

5

Report to NPCI & Your Bank

File a complaint at npci.org.in or call NPCI Helpline: 1800-120-1740.
